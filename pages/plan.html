What is my plan?

The plan

What is the use of a plan without describing to you my goal first? My goal is simple: I'm going to become a recognized SaaS entrepreneur. I'll be among the likes of MJ Demarco, Rob Walling, Pieter Levels, Steve Jobs, and all the other SaaS titans in my eyes.

Why do I want this?

Firstly, I want freedom. I want the freedom that making American dollars from an online venture brings a man. Besides being able to sustain a wealthy lifestyle in a third-world country, I want to be rich by American standards. My ego is involved in the latter. I want to get retribution for Darwin of the past.

I remember all of the heart ache I went through:

  • Feeling the need to run away from school senior year of high school because I couldn't stand the life I was leading
  • Being bullied by my peer for having run away and come back with a tail between my legs
  • Having a close friend go for my ex-girlfriend behind my back
  • Feeling like a loser because I'm financially behind all of my high-school friends who've finished college
  • Having my parents not believe in my ability to make money on my own
  • Rejection from women

So now I'm on my vengeance arc. The only revenge I know to be worth-while is success

This is journey the journey I'll take to get to where I need to go:
